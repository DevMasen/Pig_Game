How to play 👀

Welcome to Pig Game 🐽. You should play with your friend.
In this game you roll the dice as much as you want and the scores
from the dice add to your current score and you could hold it to the
main score whenever you want, But if the dice rolls to "one"
your current score will be 0 and the player will change.


How to Win 🎉

If your scores reach 100 You will be the winnnneeeeer! 🥳
Have fun and enjoy the game! 🤗
